How to Start Investing

Here are some simple steps to help you embark on your investing journey:

  1. Research
    • What stocks interest you?
    • What global events could possibly affect your portfolio?
    • Which investments best suit your risk-level?
  2. Decide
    • Is now a good time?
    • How long will I invest for?
    • How much money am I willing to risk?
  3. Choose a Broker
    • What fees do they charge?
    • Will my funds be managed?
    • Is this firm trustworthy?
    • What does their history/reviews say about the experience?

Here are some things to remember:

  1. Don't Panic!
    • The stock market can be scary, trust in your research
    • Emotions are normal, but don't make rash decisions
    • The less you follow the crowd, the more opportunities you will have
  2. The market favours those who are patient
    • Historically, the stock market has gone up approx. 10% per year
    • The stock market has always recovered eventually after even economic disasters
